Programs

Oregon Harbor of Hope supports innovative programs that address the complex needs of people facing housing insecurity, trauma, and systemic barriers. From creative healing to housing solutions, each program we help develop is rooted in compassion, dignity, and the belief that everyone deserves a path forward.

Better Neighbors

Better Neighbors is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping marginalized people, specifically those affected by the prison system and in need of assistance with addiction recovery.

Our goal is to improve transition opportunities and help create safe places that provide learning and growing opportunities for people in this demographic.

creatingbetterneighbors.org

Art Inside Out

Art Inside Out (AIO) uses creativity as a tool for connection, self-discovery, and growth. By engaging the mind through art, we make space for meaningful guidance to take hold.

Our evidence-based programs reach youth, elders, recovery groups, and more—because real change happens when content connects.
