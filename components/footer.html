Oregon Harbor of Hope is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to developing innovative programs and facilities that offer compassionate, community-driven systems of care. We focus on addressing the broad and complex needs of marginalized individuals, including those experiencing chronic homelessness, trauma, and systemic barriers-by supporting initiatives that foster stability, dignity, and long-term change.

If you have the means, please consider donating to our cause, it really makes a difference in helping us build our system that positively affects the struggling people in our city.

Please reach out to us for any information, help, or comments you may have. We look forward to hearing from you!
